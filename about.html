About our History

Founded in 2012, 101 Bagels and Subs started as a small, family-owned café just a short walk from the scenic coastline of Oceanside, California. Our founders, longtime locals with a passion for good food and community, wanted to create a place where everyone—from early-morning surfers to weekend families—could enjoy a fresh, satisfying meal made with care. Inspired by the classic flavors of East Coast bagel shops and the laid-back spirit of Southern California, we opened our doors with one simple goal: to serve the best bagels and sandwiches in town. Over the years, our commitment to quality ingredients, friendly service, and consistency has helped us grow from a humble neighborhood spot into a local favorite. Today, 101 Bagels and Subs is proud to be known as one of the best places in Oceanside to grab a hearty breakfast, a freshly made sub, or a quick coffee before heading out to enjoy the California sunshine.
